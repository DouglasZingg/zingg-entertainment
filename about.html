About Me

I’m a tools and pipeline focused developer with a background in game development, focused on building reliable systems that support artists and production teams.

I studied game development at Full Sail University and worked on multiple team based projects, including graphics programming and full game production. Through that work, I gravitated toward the technical side of production, building tools that validate assets, automate repetitive steps, and reduce downstream pipeline issues.

My portfolio reflects that focus: asset validators, publishers, packagers, editor utilities, and workflow tools designed around real production problems rather than isolated demos.

How I Approach Tools

  • Catch problems early with validation and clear feedback
  • Make pipeline standards explicit and repeatable
  • Favor predictable behavior over one off scripts
  • Design tools that fit naturally into existing workflows

What I’m Looking For

I’m seeking tools, pipeline, or technical roles at game or VFX studios, particularly in Vancouver, where I can contribute to production ready systems and collaborate closely with artists and engineers.
